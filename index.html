About me

Hello, I'm Ziad Hassan, a student majoring in Information Technology at the Borg El Arab University of Technology. My journey into the world of programming began with a deep passion for technology and a strong interest in crafting innovative web experiences.
I specialize in Full Stack Development, where I've honed my programming skills using PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind , Bootstrap,and Laravel, along with managing databases using MySQL and developing APIs. Designing and implementing web projects is my passion, and I enjoy creating attractive user interfaces and robust systems alike.

This gives me a profound understanding of future tech and how it can be applied to enhance people’s lives.
I'm always ready for new challenges and eager to contribute to innovative tech projects. Software development isn't just my career; it's my passion, and I'm constantly striving to improve and develop my skills for the ever-evolving world of technology.
